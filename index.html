This page features a DEMO of the data collection process used in the project 'Deep Learning for Language Assessment', as detailed in the paper "Logging Keystrokes in Writing by English Leaners". By clicking on the COPY-TEXT TASK button, you will be redirected to the copy-text task page utilized in this study. Clicking on the ESSAY-WRITING TASK button will take you to an essay task page. This page is a random selection from a set of 10 'just for fun' prompts provided by the English Learning platform Write&Improve. The content of those pages is the exact content that the participants in this study would be exposed to. The content on these pages is identical to what study participants encountered. Your keystrokes will be recorded and stored by your browser while you type in the textbox on these pages. Please note that your data is secure and not stored by us. After completing your typing, you can download a JSON file containing your keystrokes by clicking the download button located at the bottom of the page.
